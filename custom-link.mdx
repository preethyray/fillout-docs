## How to customize your form's share link

You can change your share link even without a [custom domain](/custom-domains). On the **Share** page of your form, go to **Customize link**. Then, click `Edit link`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at5.38.28PM.png)
</Frame>

Choose `Pretty URL`. If you prefer using your own custom domain (completely white-labelled), click [here](/custom-domains) (requires Fillout business or enterprise).

<Frame style={{ width: '432px' , height: '261px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at5.37.31PM.png)
</Frame>

Then, enter your **subdomain** and click `Change my subdomain`. That’s it\!

<Check>
  **Good to know:** The original default form link will still redirect to the same form. There is no need to share the new customized link to those whom you have shared the original link with prior.
</Check>

## Edit the custom ending of your link

You can enter any text for your form link’s ending to personalize it. In this example, we'll use happiness-survey. Type in the ending and click `Set custom ending`.

<Frame style={{ width: '480px' , height: '297px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at5.43.41PM.png)
</Frame>

That's it\! Now, your form link is now at [foodmart.fillout.com/happiness-survey](http://foodmart.fillout.com/happiness-survey).

## Customize social media share preview

By default, your form's share preview shows the name of your form and a general description. To change it, check out this quick [guide](/customize-form-share-link).

## How to reset your subdomain

Click `Edit link` followed by **Show default link**. Then, click `Change subdomain`. This will remove the subdomain from **all your forms**.

<Frame style={{ width: '480px' , height: '303px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at5.38.17PM.png)
</Frame>

Alternatively, you can go to **General** under account **Settings** to remove it.
