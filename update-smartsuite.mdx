## How it works

### **1. Switch your form to update mode**

First, choose `SmartSuite` on the **Integrate** tab.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-17at10.37.22AM.png)
</Frame>

Then specify that you want to `Update record`.

<Frame style={{ width: '576px', height: '231px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-17at10.37.32AM.png)
</Frame>

### **2. Use the formula to specify the page to update**

You'll now get a formula that defines a **unique link** for every record in your table. In SmartSuite, create a new `formula`field and paste the formula Fillout provides.

<Frame style={{ width: '336px', height: '352px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/ScreenShot2023-10-19at5.51.27PM.png)
</Frame>

### **3. Preview your form**

That's it\! Click `Finish setup`. Try out your form in `Preview` mode, or click one of your unique links to update a record after youâ€™ve published your form\!

Your form is **automatically pre-filled** for whichever record it is updating.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Inv.png)
</Frame>
