## Overview

Sometimes you may need to reset your password for various reasons, including forgotten it, security concerns, or as a precautionary measure after potential unauthorized access to your account. It's also recommended to update passwords regularly for better online security.

### How to reset your password

On **Fillout**'s **homepage**, click `Log in` on the upper right corner. Alternatively, you may go to https://build.fillout.com/login. Then, click `Forgot password?`.

<Frame>
  ![How to reset your password](/images/account/image-21.png)
</Frame>

Enter your **email address** and `Send reset link`. Then, go to your email inbox, and click the **reset link**.

<Frame>
  ![How to reset your password](/images/account/image-22.png)
</Frame>

A new tab will open where you will be asked to `Change password`. That's it\! You'll now be logged in to your account. If you signed up using Gmail, the same steps above apply.
