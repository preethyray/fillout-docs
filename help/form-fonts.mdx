## How to change the default font

Click `Theme` to open the **Form Designer**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form Designer](/images/style-and-branding/image-1.png)
</Frame>

On the left-hand panel of your screen, click `Font`. Choose between thousands of available Google fonts.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form Designer](/images/style-and-branding/image-2.png)
</Frame>

### How to u**pload a custom font**

You can also use any font outside the font library. Click `Custom` to add a new font.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form Designer](/images/style-and-branding/image-3.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  This feature is available on **Pro**, **Business**, and **Enterprise** plans. Learn more about our pricing plans and advanced features [**here**](https://www.fillout.com/pricing).
</Note>
