## Edit form access

Go to the **Settings** tab in the top menu , and click `Access`. Toggle the features below as you see fit.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-32.png" />
</Frame>

### **Close form**

This immediately disables your form (after clicking `Save`) and prevents new submissions. However, you can still continue to edit your form and test it in `Preview` mode.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-33.png" />
</Frame>

You can also enable `Custom form closed message` to edit the default message that respondents see when a form is no longer accessible.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-34.png" />
</Frame>

### Form open date

Choose a date and time for when you want to start accepting responses.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-35.png" />
</Frame>

### Form expiration date

Set a date and time for when you want to stop accepting responses.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-36.png" />
</Frame>

### Form submission limit

You can also opt to disable your form upon receiving a certain number of responses.

<Frame>
<img src="/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-37.png" />
</Frame>
