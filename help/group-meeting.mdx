## What is a group meeting

Group scheduling allows multiple participants to join the same call hosted by a single person. This is ideal for webinars, courses, and other group events.

## How to set up

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect your calendar">

Click `Scheduling` in the top menu of the homepage followed by `Calendars and apps`. `+ Connect calendar` and log in to your Google Account.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/scheduling/image-39.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Create a scheduling form">

Click `Scheduling links` in the left-hand panel followed by `+ Scheduling form`. Select `Group meeting`.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/scheduling/image-40.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Configure event details">

Set the meeting **Title** and **Event duration**. Optionally, set your [Availability](/help/fillout-scheduling-availability) through the left-hand panel.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/scheduling/image-41.png" />
</Frame>

You can also customize avatars or use a generic image such as a company logo for the event to maintain brand consistency by clicking the avatar on the form or by going to the `Booking page`.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/scheduling/image-42.png" />
</Frame>

That’s it! Your scheduling link is now ready to go. `Preview` then `Publish` your form, and send the link or embed it anywhere.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Set the maximum attendee limit

In the left-hand panel, add your preferred number of **Max attendees per event**.
