## How to add a cover page

Click `+ Add page` followed by `Cover`. Then, enter the text.

<Frame>
  ![Add a cover page](/images/c1.webp)
</Frame>

When the form is opened, the text will **animate smoothly**. You can also change the **Button text** through the left-hand panel.

## Customize your cover page

Change the **Custom cover page layout** through the left-hand panel. Check out this [guide](/help/layout). You can also add a small **Image above title** through the same panel.

<Frame>
  ![Customize your cover page](/images/c2.webp)
</Frame>

Customize the **design** of your page and add a [logo](/help/logo) by clicking `Theme` in the upper left corner of the page. Learn more [here](/help/themes).
