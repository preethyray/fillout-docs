## How to copy and paste form fields

Select the field you want to copy. Then click `⌘ + C` or `Ctrl + C`. Alternatively, you may click its `settings icon`.

<Frame>
  ![Copy and paste a question](/images/guides/image-64.png)
</Frame>

This will open up the right-hand panel. Click `⋮` or the vertical ellipsis followed by `Copy`.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/guides/image-65.png)
</Frame>

Then, paste anywhere using `⌘ + V` or `Ctrl + V`.

<Check>
  **Good to know:** You can paste questions and fields on different pages of your form or even on entirely different forms.
</Check>

## Copy and paste a page

Click `⋮` or the vertical ellipsis next to the name of the page followed by `Copy`. Then, paste anywhere using `⌘ + V` or `Ctrl + V`.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/guides/image-66.png)
</Frame>

Alternatively, you may also click `Duplicate`. From here, you will also be able to `Rename page` or `Delete page`.
