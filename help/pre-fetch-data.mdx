## How to p**ull external information into your form**

### **1. Select your integration**

Navigate to the **Integrate** page from the top menu and choose an integration.

### 2. Add a pre-fetch record

Then, click `+ Add new` under **Pre-fetch records**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/Screenshot2025-02-14at11.33.56AM.png" alt="Screenshot of the Fillout form creation page" />
</Frame>

Enter a **name** for your record, then select a **base** and a **table**. Under **Filter by**, choose an Airtable field to match with a [URL parameter](/help/url-parameters) or static text. In this example, we'll select email.

</Frame>
