## What is collective scheduling

Collective scheduling allows a user to book meetings or appointments with two or more hosts at the same time. Fillout Scheduling pools the availability of all hosts to find a suitable meeting time.

## How to set up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect calendars">
    Team members have to link their calendar to Fillout. In the `Scheduling` page, click `Calendars` on the left-hand panel followed by `+ Connect calendar` and sign in with Google.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/scheduling/image-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the scheduling form">
    Click `Scheduling links` on the left-hand panel followed by `+ Scheduling form`. Choose `Collective meeting` and select which team members will participate.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/scheduling/image-3.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure event details">
    Set the **Title** and **Event duration**. You can also customize avatars or use a generic image such as a company logo for the event to maintain brand consistency in the `Booking page` from left-hand panel, or simply hover over it.

    <Frame>
      ![](/images/scheduling/image-4.png)
    </Frame>
    That's it\! `Publish` your form and share the link or embed it anywhere.
  </Step>
</Steps>
