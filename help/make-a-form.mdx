## How to build a form in minutes

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up or log in">
    Sign up [here](https://app.fillout.com/signup) or log in [here](https://app.fillout.com/login) using any email or your Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a form type">
    On your **home** page, click `+ Make a Fillout` , then choose between a **Blank form**, **Template**, or **Connect** with 3rd party app integrations like Airtable, Notion, Hubspot, and more.

    <Frame>
      ![Choose a form type](/images/maf1.webp)
    </Frame>
    If you’re unsure about which to use, choose `Blank form`. You can always connect to an [integration](/help/integrations) later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize the design">
    Click `Theme` to pick from one of the pre-made themes or [customize your own](/help/themes).

    <Frame>
      ![Customize the design](/images/maf2.webp)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add questions or fields">
    Click or drag and drop your desired [field types](/help/question-types) from the left-hand panel. Choose between over 40 different types.

    <Frame>
      ![Add questions or fields](/images/maf3.webp)
    </Frame>
    Then, enter the text and edit it in-line. Click its `settings icon` to customize it in the right-hand panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish your form">
    `Preview` your form to test it. Once everything's good to go, press `Publish`.

    <Frame>
      ![Publish your form](/images/4.webp)
    </Frame>
    That’s it\! Share your **form link** anywhere or **embed your form** on any website. To view form submissions, head over to [**Results**](/help/view-responses) in the top menu.
  </Step>
</Steps>
