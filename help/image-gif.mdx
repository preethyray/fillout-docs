## How to add an image or GIF to a form page

Click `Image` in the field choices or drag and drop this to your form. Next, click **Change image** in the center of your screen.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig1.webp)
</Frame>

You can select an image from the **Unsplash**, copy and paste an image's **Link** or **Upload** an image from your desktop.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig2.webp)
</Frame>

To change the **size** and **alignment** of your image, click on the image followed by the `settings` icon to open the right-hand panel.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig3.webp)
</Frame>

## How to set an image as your background

Open the **Form designer** by clicking `Theme`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig4.webp)
</Frame>

In the left-hand panel, under **Position**, click `Add image`. All of the form positions except for the top left form position allow adding a background image.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig5.webp)
</Frame>

**Upload** the image from your desktop, choose an image from the **Unsplash** or \*\*\*\*paste the an image's **Link**.

Note that your image will be shown on every page, except for cover pages (if applicable). You can edit the brightness of your image in the left-hand panel. To adjust, hover over the image and click `Position`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig6.webp)
</Frame>

<Note>
  For full page image, generally 16:9 is best or 1280 x 720. For side-by-side layouts, 512 x 720 is the ideal size, however you can use any size because the positioning can be adjusted.
</Note>

## How to let respondents choose from images

Click the `Picture choice` field or drag and drop it onto your form. Next, hover over one of the choices and click **Edit**followed by **Change image**. Repeat this for the rest of the choices.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig7.webp)
</Frame>

You can change each image’s **size** in the right-hand panel by clicking the `settings` icon. In the right-hand panel, go to **Layout**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig8.webp)
</Frame>

You can also change the color of the choices background with the **Option background color** under **Basic**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/ig9.webp)
</Frame>
