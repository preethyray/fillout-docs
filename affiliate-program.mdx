## How to join

Create a [Fillout account](https://build.fillout.com/signup?ref=fillouthelparticle-affiliateprogram), if you haven't already. Next, visit our [**Affiliate program page**](https://www.fillout.com/affiliate-program?ref=fillouthelparticle-affiliateprogram), and click `Start earning today`.

<Frame>
  ![How to join](/images/account/image-19.png)
</Frame>

In the popup window, click `Become an affiliate`. Then, enter your details, which will be used for affiliate payouts.

<Frame style={{ width: '432px' , height: '376px' }}>
  ![How to join](/images/account/image-20.png)
</Frame>

That's it! Now you'll be able share your **unique referral link** via messaging, email, socials, and more. You can check your **affiliate dashboard** to see how many users subscribed or upgraded to a paid plan through your referral.
