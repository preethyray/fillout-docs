## What Shopify is

[Shopify](https://www.shopify.com/) is a leading e-commerce platform that allows businesses to easily create and manage their own online stores. With customizable templates, integrated payment options, and powerful tools for marketing and inventory management, Shopify helps entrepreneurs grow and scale their businesses globally.

## Add a form to your Shopify page

After creating your form in Fillout, click `Publish` in the upper right corner. Then, navigate to the **Share** tab in the top menu and choose an embed type. Click `Use this embed` to copy the code.

<Frame style={{ width: '576px' , height: '152px' }}>
  ![](/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-12.png)
</Frame>

Log in to your Shopify store and click `Online Store` followed by `Pages`. Then, choose the page where you would like to insert the form into.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-13.png)
</Frame>

Click the Show HTML `</>` icon. Then, paste the code in the **Content** field.

<Frame style={{ width: '480px' , height: '270px' }}>
  ![](/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-14.png)
</Frame>

Click `Save` in the lower right corner to preview the page. That's it\! Now, you'll be able to see responses under the [**Results**]() tab of your Fillout form.

<Frame style={{ width: '576px' , height: '317px' }}>
  ![](/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-15.png)
</Frame>

## Add a form to all pages

Select `Themes` in the left menu under `Online Store`. Then, click the horizontal ellipsis or `...` and select `Edit code`.

<Frame style={{ width: '528px' , height: '264px' }}>
  ![](/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-16.png)
</Frame>

Go to `</> theme.liquid` from the left-hand panel and paste the [**embed code**](/shopify) of your Fillout form at the top of \</body\> and \</html\> tags at the bottom and click `Save` in the upper right corner.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/sharing-and-embedding/image-17.png)
</Frame>

That's it\! Your embedded form will now show up on every page of your Shopify website.
