## What is a favicon

A **favicon**, short for "favorite icon," is a small image or icon associated with a website. It is displayed in the browser's address bar, next to the page title in browser tabs, and can also be shown in bookmarks and history lists.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Settings tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at5.14.48PM.png)
</Frame>

## How to add a favicon

From your workspace, click your **Account name** in the bottom right corner followed by `Settings`. In the left-hand panel, click `General`. Under **Custom favicon**, click `Add image`.

<Frame style={{ width: '528px' , height: '332px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Settings tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at5.16.05PM.png)
</Frame>

**Upload** your logo or icon. Alternatively, you can also copy and paste an image **link**. Then, click `Set image`.

Lastly, click `Save changes` at the bottom. You're good to go\! Your logo or icon will now appear on your forms' browser tabs and links.
