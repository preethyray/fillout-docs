## Leave a comment

Open your form, and go to the **Results** page. Click a response you’d like to leave a feedback or question on followed by `Add comment`.

<Frame>
  ![Leave a comment](/images/account/image-25.png)
</Frame>

Alternatively, you can hover over the second column from the left and click the `expand` icon.

To `Edit` or `Delete` a comment, click `...` or the horizontal ellipsis icon.

<Frame>
  ![Leave a comment](/images/account/image-26.png)
</Frame>

## Mention a team member

In the comment box, tag a user by typing `@`, then select a [team member](/invite-collaborators) in the dropdown.

<Frame>
  ![Leave a comment](/images/account/image-27.png)
</Frame>

That’s it\! The user you tagged will now receive a notification via email.

### Unsubscribe to comments

In the **Comments** panel, simply click `Unsubscribe`.

<Frame>
  ![Leave a comment](/images/account/image-28.png)
</Frame>
