## How to change your form's theme

On your form, click `Theme` to open the **Form designer**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form](/images/th1.webp)
</Frame>

Select from any of the `Featured` or past themes. Previous form designs are saved under under `My themes`.

<Frame style={{ width: '192px' , height: '336px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form](/images/th2.webp)
</Frame>

## How to customize your chosen theme

Click `Custom theme` to modify visual elements, and tailor the theme to your liking.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form](/images/images/the3.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** This feature is available to **Starter** users and above. For more details on pricing, click [here](https://www.fillout.com/pricing).
</Note>

The following are the elements you can customize in the left-hand panel:

- **Background** color (for [container over background layout](/layout))
- **Questions background** color
- **Primary** color of buttons and accents throughout the form
- **Questions** text color
- **Answers** text color
- **Font** style

<Check>
  **Good to know:** You can choose from any Google font or [upload your own font](/form-fonts).
</Check>

Highlight text to change its font size, style, or color.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form](/images/th4.webp)
</Frame>

You can also add and adjust:

- [**Logo**](/logo) for a professional and polished look
- [**Position**](/layout) or layout of your form
- [**Image**](/image-gif) uploaded or linked to the form
- **Brightness** of the image
- **Questions** button size
- **Alignment** of questions

  ![Screenshot of the Theme tab of a form](/images/th5.webp)
</Frame>
