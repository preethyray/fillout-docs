## Automatically redirect to any form

Go to your form's [**Ending page**](/ending-page) and choose the `New form` on the left-hand panel. Then, select the new form you want to redirect users to in the drop-down.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logic/image-22.png)
</Frame>

## Pass data between forms with URL parameters

First, make sure you've registered the allowed URL parameters in the form you are sending users to. Details [here](/url-parameters). Go to **Settings** followed by `URL parameters`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logic/image-23.png)
</Frame>

Next, `Set URL parameters` through the left side of the form and map fields.

<Frame style={{ width: '528px' , height: '255px' }}>
 ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logic/image-24.png)
</Frame>

That's it\! Make sure to `Publish` both forms when you're ready to go live.

## **Let users re-submit a form**

Go to your form's **Ending**, and drag and drop a `Fill again` button from the left side.

<Frame style={{ width: '288px' , height: '124px' }}>
 ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logic/image-25.png)
</Frame>

By default, the form will restart from the first step. Enable the `Start from page` switch in the right side to choose a specific page to restart from.

<Frame style={{ width: '288px' , height: '226px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logic/image-26.png)
</Frame>
