## How to add a logo

Open your form and click `Theme`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logo1.webp)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** This feature is available to **Starter** users and above. For more details on pricing, click [**here**](https://www.fillout.com/pricing).
</Note>

Toggle the switch next to **Logo**, and click `Add logo`. **Upload** the logo file from your desktop or **drag and drop** it to the pop-up window. You can also choose to copy and paste the **Link** of your logo instead.

<Frame style={{ width: '240px' , height: '87px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logo2.webp)
</Frame>

<Check>
  **Good to know:** When a logo is added, it will automatically appear in all pages except for the cover page. There is no need to upload it manually per page.
</Check>

To change your logo, hover over it, and click **Change image**. To hide it, untoggle the **Logo** switch.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/logo3.webp)
</Frame>
