## How to add a heading

Open your form and click `Heading` in the left-hand panel or simply drag and drop it into to your form. Then, type the text you want to display.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Form designer tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-25at4.16.25PM.png)
</Frame>

By default, your header will be colored according to your formâ€™s theme. To customize your heading, highlight the `header text`. You can change the size, color and alignment of the text.

You can also [include links](/add-links-to-form) in your header.
