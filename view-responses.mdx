## How to view submissions

Open your form, and go to **Results** from the top menu. Sort responses or hide columns by hovering over a column header, followed by clicking <Icon icon="caret-down" iconType="solid" color="#FFC738" size="26" /> the dropdown icon.

<Frame>
  ![View responses](/images/15.webp)
</Frame>

`Filter` responses to narrow responses based on a specific preference. Use `Date range` to set a range for submission/edit dates.

### <Icon icon="keyboard" iconType="solid" color="#FFC738" size="22" /> **Shortcuts**

- `⌘ + F` or `Ctrl + F` while clicking on any cell to search through your entries
- `⌘ + C` or `Ctrl + C` on a cell to copy its contents
- `Double-click any cell` to enlarge its contents (helpful for long inputs)

## View individual submissions

Hover over a cell and click the `expand icon` to view the answers and submission details. Here, you can [comment and tag](/comment) your teammates.

<Frame>
  ![View individual submissions](/images/16.webp)
</Frame>

## Export submissions

Click the <Icon icon="download" iconType="solid" color="#FFC738" size="20" /> download icon in the top right to export all of your responses in a formatted CSV respective of the filters.

<Frame style={{ width: '288px' , height: '89px' }}>
  ![Export submissions](/images/17.webp)
</Frame>

## Check misconfiguration errors

Fillout alerts you of any misconfiguration errors. For example, you recently changed a field type in your Airtable/Notion database, but forgot to change it in Fillout.
<Frame style={{ width: '480px' , height: '137px' }}>
  ![Check misconfiguration errors](/images/18.webp)
</Frame>

Click `error icon` to view the error message. You can also enable [error notifications](/email-notifications) to get notified via email.
