## Design your form

Click `Theme` to open the form designer, where you can edit the colors, font, and position of your current [theme](/themes). To change this, go to `All themes`.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/scheduling/image-15.png)
</Frame>

To make [advanced customizations](/advanced-designer), scroll down in the left-hand panel to open the `Advanced designer`.

## Edit form link

Click the **Share** tab of your form followed by **Customize link** to `Edit link`. You can also **Change share preview** here and add an [image and/or description](/custom-link).

<Frame style={{ width: '528px' , height: '320px' }}>
  ![](/images/scheduling/image-16.png)
</Frame>

Paid Fillout accounts can also [add a custom domain](/custom-domains) for fully branded scheduling forms.

## Change your avatar

Simply hover over the `avatar` in your form to customize it. Alternatively, you may do so in the [booking page](/how-to-create-scheduling-form#1590ee47667b80718a29f72c05c84a8d).

<Frame style={{ width: '288px' , height: '81px' }}>
  ![](/images/scheduling/image-17.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** The new avatar will show up on all of your Fillout forms and scheduling forms.
</Note>
