## What is Tag Manager

[Google Tag Manager](https://tagmanager.google.com/?ref=fillout-forms-help-article) is a free tool that allows you to manage and deploy marketing tags on your website without editing the code. It simplifies tracking and analytics for better insights and optimization.

## How it works

### 1. Link your Fillout form to Tag Manager

First, open your Fillout form or create a new one. Then, navigate to **Integrate** and look for `Tag Manager`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-18at2.18.58PM.png)
</Frame>

`Connect a custom domain` to get started. Follow the quick instructions in the next screen to enter your **Google Tag Manager container ID**.

<Note>
  **Note:** Only admins will be able to add a custom domain.
</Note>

### 2. Add triggers

In your Tag Manager **Workspace**, head over to `Variables` from the left panel and click `Configure`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2025-02-18at2.21.34PM.png)
</Frame>

`Tick` all variables under **Clicks** and **Forms**.

<Frame style={{ width: '336px', height: '351px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2024-08-28at5.08.24PM.png)
</Frame>

Then, go back to your **Workspace**. Click `Tags` in the left panel followed by `New`. In **Tag Configuration**, we’ll choose Google Analytics. Copy and paste the [**Measurement ID**](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/12270356?hl=en) and enter an **Event Name**.

<Frame style={{ width: '624px', height: '243px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2024-08-28at5.28.05PM.png)
</Frame>

Click `Save` in the upper right corner, then `Add Trigger`. Click `+` on the right corner and set your preferences. In our example, we’ll fire the trigger when respondents click the submit button in the Fillout form.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Integrate tab of a form](/images/Screenshot2024-08-28at5.41.45PM.png)
</Frame>

`Preview` the setup in your Google Tag Manager. Once everything’s good, click `Submit`. Then, go back to your Fillout form and `Publish` it.

### 3. Publish and share your form.

Once you’re done click `Finish setup` and `Publish your form`.
