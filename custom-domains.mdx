## What is a custom domain

A **custom domain** is a unique web address to replace your web form's default URL, enhancing brand visibility and trust. It adds professionalism, improves SEO, and ensures a seamless user experience.

## How to add a custom domain

On the **Share** page of your form, click `Custom domain`.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/ucd1.webp)
</Frame>

Alternatively, you can go to your account **Settings** from the homepage and click `Custom domains`. Enter a **subdomain** like [forms.mydomainname.com](http://forms.mydomainname.com) or [surveys.mydomainname.com](http://surveys.mydomainname.com).

<Frame style={{ width: '432px' , height: '220px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/ucd2.webp)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Note:** This feature is available on **Business**, and **Enterprise** plans. Learn more about our pricing plans and advanced features [**here**](https://www.fillout.com/pricing).
</Note>

Log in to your domain provider, like Godaddy, Cloudflare, NameCheap, Netlify or another website where you bought your domain. Then, follow the instructions below.

<Card title="How to add the DNS record:" icon="plus" iconType="solid" color="#FFC738">
  1. Log in to your domain provider.
  2. Add the CNAME record as instructed in the Fillout dashboard.
  3. Wait until the DNS records are verified (this can take anywhere from a few seconds to a few hours). Click Refresh in Fillout to check if your domain has been connected.
</Card>

You'll know the setup is complete once you see a green checkmark icon <Icon icon="check" iconType="solid" color="#FFC738"/>.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/ucd3.webp)
</Frame>

Now, all your form links will begin with your **domain name**. You can see this in the **Share** page.

<Frame style={{ width: '576px' , height: '168px' }}>
  ![Screenshot of the Share tab of a form](/images/ucd4.webp)
</Frame>

You can also [edit the ending of your link](/custom-link).

<Check>
  **Good to know:** Fillout automatically provisions an SSL certificate for you, to ensure your forms stay secure.
</Check>
